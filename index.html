App Description

ML ASL Speech To Rec is an innovative application designed to bridge the communication gap between non-ASL users and individuals who are deaf or hard of hearing. Utilizing advanced machine learning models and real-time processing, the app translates sign language gestures captured via the camera into text, and spoken words captured via the microphone into text, facilitating seamless communication. This app empowers users to communicate effectively, ensuring inclusivity and accessibility for all. With its user-friendly interface and cutting-edge technology, ML ASL Speech To Rec is a must-have tool for anyone looking to enhance their communication skills and connect with the deaf and hard of hearing community.
